Are you proposing as well to provide the hardware and Internet connection for these boxes?

I know of one person at least who runs a node that tracks the C-Lightning master (I think they do a nightly build?), and I run a node that I update every release of C-Lightning (and runs CLBOSS as well).
I do not know the actual implementations of what they connect to, but LND is very popular on the network and LNBIG is known to be an LND shop, and LNBIG is so pervasive that nearly every long-lived forwarding node has at least one channel with *some* LNBIG node.
I consider this "good enough" in practice to catch interop bugs, but some interop bugs are deeper than just direct node-to-node communications.
For example, we had bugs in our interop with LND `keysend` before, by my memory.
