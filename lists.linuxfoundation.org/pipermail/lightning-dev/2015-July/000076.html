It occurs to me that with the LN, it might be practical to outsource SPV proofs from other nodes.

It is obviously ridiculously prohibitive and self-defeating to include SPV proofs in the blockchain…however, in practice this would only be necessary when the SPV client fails to pay the prover.

Thoughts?
