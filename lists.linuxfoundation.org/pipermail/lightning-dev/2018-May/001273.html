Hello everyone,

Join the MIT Digital Currency Initiative and Fidelity Labs on May 18-19 for a two-day event on Layer 2. Attendees will hear from leading experts in the field, and have a chance to develop applications during an all-day hackathon at the MIT Media Lab. For more details, check this website: http://www.bostonblockchaincommunity.com/

Day 1
Where: Fidelity Labs
When: 5/18 1:00pm-6:00pm
What: Lively talks and panels followed by a reception

Day 2
Where: Media Lab, 6th Floor
When: 5/19 9:00am-8:00pm
What: Hackathon, with breakfast, lunch and a reception included

This event is free and open to all! Register here: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/l2-summit-tickets-45235002109

We hope to see you there!
